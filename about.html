About Medical Career College of Health Science and Technology, Jos

Shaping the Future of Healthcare Education

Medical Career College of Health Science and Technology, Jos, is located at No. 2 Rikkos, Jos by TT and T Junction. Our institution is a forward-thinking college dedicated to training highly skilled, competent, and ethically grounded health professionals.

Situated in the serene and culturally rich city of Jos, we have grown into a respected centre of excellence in healthcare education, committed to preparing the next generation of practitioners who will shape the future of healthcare delivery in Nigeria and beyond.

Academic Excellence

We offer a dynamic blend of theoretical instruction, hands-on practical training, and real clinical exposure. This approach enables students to acquire the confidence and competence required for today's evolving healthcare needs. Our experienced faculty members provide close mentorship, ensuring that each student receives the guidance, discipline, and support needed to excel.
